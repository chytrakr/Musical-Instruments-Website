ORCHESTRA::

An Orchestra is a group of instruments that plays a music written for a combination of instruments. the number and types of instruments included in the orchestra depends on the style of music being played. the modern orchestra is made up of four section instruments-Stringed(ex.Violin,Guitar),woodwind,drums and brass.
